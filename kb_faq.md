## Appointment Booking

### How to Book
Appointments can be booked through:
- Website chatbot
- Phone call to reception
- In-person at reception

Required Information:
- Patient Name
- Mobile Number
- Preferred Doctor or Specialization
- Preferred Date and Time

### Arrival Policy
- Patients must arrive 15 minutes before appointment time.
- Bring identification and previous medical reports if available.

### Rescheduling Policy
- Rescheduling is allowed up to 4 hours before the appointment.
- Subject to doctor availability.

### Cancellation Policy
- Cancellations allowed up to 4 hours before appointment.
- Refund policy depends on medical center regulations.

---

## Doctor Availability
- Doctor schedules are updated in real time.
- Available time slots are shown during booking.
- If a slot is unavailable, the next available slot will be suggested.

## Safety and Medical Advice Policy
- The assistant does NOT provide diagnosis.
- The assistant does NOT prescribe medication.
- For emergency symptoms (chest pain, breathing difficulty, heavy bleeding, fainting):
  - Call 1990 immediately or visit the nearest emergency unit.

---

## Human Agent Handover Policy
The system will transfer to a human agent when:
- User explicitly requests a human
- Medical diagnosis or prescription is requested
- Emergency symptoms are mentioned
- Information is not available in this knowledge base

---

## Privacy Policy
- Do not share highly sensitive medical information in chat.
- Chat logs may be stored for up to 30 days.
- Sensitive details may be masked for security.
